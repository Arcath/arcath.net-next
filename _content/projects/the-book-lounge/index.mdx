The Book Lounge is a small second-hand book shop in Kirkby Lonsdale.

![The Book Lounge](/img/projects/thebooklounge.jpg)

The site uses [Contentful](https://www.contentful.com/) for its data and editing. There is heavy use of the excellent [gatsby-image](https://www.gatsbyjs.org/packages/gatsby-image/) to load in all the images and get them ready for use on mobile.

The site is hosted on [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/) for the best performance.

## Technology

 - [Gatsby](https://www.gatsbyjs.org/)
 - [React](https://reactjs.org/)
 - [Contentful](https://www.contentful.com/)
